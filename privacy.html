Privacy Policy

ElectroSolve respects the privacy of its users. This application does not collect, store, or share any personal information such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, or passwords. The calculations performed in ElectroSolve are processed locally within the application or through the web interface. Any values entered by users (such as voltage or resistance) are used only for calculation purposes and are not saved or transmitted to any external servers. ElectroSolve may display third-party advertisements in the future to support development and maintenance of the application. These advertisements may use standard technologies such as cookies to display relevant ads. ElectroSolve does not control how third-party services collect or use data. Users are advised to review the privacy policies of third-party services used within the app, such as advertising platforms. By using ElectroSolve, you agree to this privacy policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the application. If you have any questions or concerns regarding privacy, you may contact the developer through the provided project channels.
