1️⃣ Ohm’s Law

...

Formula: V = I × R

Ohm’s Law states that the current flowing through a conductor is directly proportional to the voltage applied and inversely proportional to its resistance.

Formula: V = I × R

1️⃣ joule's law

...

Formula: V = I × R

Joule’s Law explains that electrical energy is converted into heat when current flows through a resistor. The heat produced depends on current, resistance, and time.

Formula: E = I² × R × t

1️⃣ electric power

...

Formula: V = I × R

Electric power is the rate at which electrical energy is transferred or consumed. It is measured in watts.

Formula: P = V × I
